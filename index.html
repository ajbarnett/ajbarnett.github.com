The Day

Jared and Allie will be getting married on Saturday, June 22nd, 2013. Please arrive to Lionscrest Manor at 6:00 PM. Directions can be found below. The ceremony will start shortly after and will be outside. The dinner and reception will follow, also at Lionscrest. Please come prepared to show off your dancing skills.
